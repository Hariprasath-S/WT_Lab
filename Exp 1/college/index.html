GCT is an autonomous state-funded engineering college located in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India. It is affiliated to Anna University. The reputed institution had celebrated the platinum jubliee this year. It is an awesome eductional institution with affordable fee for students. 100% placement in all departments each year. The college was started by Mr. G.D.Naidu who donated the land for this college. There are UG and PG courses for maximum branches.There present a cooperative store which is the stationery store for notebooks.
